Privacy Policy

CalmParent+ — ADHD Routine Planner for Kids
Effective date: March 26, 2026

CalmParent+ ("the App") is developed and operated by Janusz Lenkiewicz, based in Poland. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and what rights you have. We designed CalmParent+ with a privacy-first approach: most of your data never leaves your device.

1. Data Collection

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the App's features.

Data stored locally on your device only

  • Child's first name (optional — you may use a nickname or leave it blank)
  • Child's age
  • Routines and schedules you create
  • Meltdown / SOS event logs
  • Parent mood check-in entries
This data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to it, cannot read it, and do not upload it to any server.

Data collected when you sign in

  • Email address — collected through Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In for account authentication

Data processed for Premium AI features

  • When you use Premium AI-powered features (e.g., personalized routine suggestions), certain data is sent to third-party AI providers (see Section 4). This data is anonymized before transmission — it does not include your name, email, or any information that directly identifies you or your child.

2. Data Use

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Your email address is used to create and manage your account, enable sign-in, and manage your subscription.
  • AI features (Premium): Anonymized routine and behavioral data is processed by AI models to generate personalized suggestions and insights.
  • App improvement: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage analytics may be collected to improve the App's performance and features.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Data Storage

  • Local data (routines, logs, mood entries, child info) is stored on your device using the operating system's secure storage. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted.
  • Account data (email) is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • AI requests are processed in real time and are not stored by CalmParent+ after the response is delivered. Refer to our AI providers' privacy policies for their data retention practices.

4. Third Parties

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — for authentication. These services share only the data you authorize (typically your email address).
  • OpenAI / Anthropic (Claude) — Premium AI features send anonymized data to these providers for processing. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for subscription management and payment processing. We do not have access to your payment details.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

CalmParent+ is designed for parents and caregivers, not for children. The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU).
  • The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • Any child-related data (name, age) is entered by the parent, stored locally on the parent's device, and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • The App complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions regarding minors.
  • If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and other applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to data processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at j.lenkiewicz@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Poland, this is the President of the Office for Personal Data Protection (UODO) — uodo.gov.pl.
